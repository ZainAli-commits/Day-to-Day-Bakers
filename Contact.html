Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

🛵 Do you offer home delivery?

+
Yes! We offer home delivery in the local area. You can place your order by contacting us on WhatsApp at +92 349 5407325 and we'll arrange delivery as soon as possible.

📅 Can I cancel or change my reservation?

+
Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation by contacting us on WhatsApp or calling us at least 2 hours before your reserved time. We'll be happy to accommodate any changes.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

+
We currently accept cash payments at our restaurant. For online orders and delivery, we also accept EasyPaisa and JazzCash mobile payments.

🎂 Can you arrange birthday or event setups?

+
Absolutely! We love making special occasions memorable. Contact us at least 24 hours in advance and we'll arrange decorations, special seating, and a customized experience for your celebration.

🍕 Are your ingredients fresh and halal?

+
Yes, 100%! All our ingredients are fresh, sourced daily, and fully halal certified. We take food quality and hygiene very seriously at Day to Day Bakers.

🏪 How many branches do you have?

+
We currently have 2 branches, both located in the Mandra area. Our second branch just opened 3 months ago due to the overwhelming love and support from our customers!
