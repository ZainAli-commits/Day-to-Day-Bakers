✨ Our Story

From Saudi Arabia to Your Table

Day to Day Bakers was founded 2 years ago by Khizar Hayat — a man with a big dream and an even bigger heart. After years of working hard in Saudi Arabia, Khizar returned home for family reasons and decided to turn his passion for food into something real.

Starting from scratch, he built Day to Day Bakers from the ground up — a place where fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and warm hospitality come together every single day. What began as a small bakery and pizza hut quickly won the hearts of the local community.

Just 3 months ago, driven by overwhelming success and customer love, Khizar proudly opened a second branch — a milestone that proves dreams do come true with dedication and hard work.
